Quick interview with Yogiri Takatou!

   

Although he may not look like much, Yogiri Takatou is a high schooler on a completely different level of power.
With his Instant Death ability, neither foes nor even the laws of physics in his new world stand a chance against him.

A quick look at our fighter's Strengths!

Yogiri's Core Abilites
  • Instant Death: Can end anything with a thought — living beings, objects, concepts, even the laws of reality. Works automatically as defense and ignores all resistance, immortality, and causality.
    •  
  • Half-Death: Can selectively “kill” specific parts (limbs, senses, organs) without killing the whole target.
    •  
  • Conceptual Erasure: Can erase abstract phenomena like gravity, time, space, memories, or abilities themselves.
    •  
  • Atemporal & Remote Death: Can kill through recordings, across dimensions, or outside normal time and space.
    •  
  • Immunity & Invulnerability Negation: Even indestructible armors, gods, or untouchable entities fall to his ability.
    •  
  • Automatic Defense: Instantly retaliates against any threat — even those faster than light or beyond time.
    •  
  • Threat Detection: Senses hostility, danger, and power sources across dimensions, and preemptively kills them.
    •  
Higher Phases / True Form
  • Gate System: Restrains his power in “phases” to avoid destroying the world; higher gates allow full conceptual erasure.
    •  
  • :Abstract Existence: His human self is just a vessel — his true form is the embodiment of The End of All Things, a being beyond fate, time, gods, and multiverses.
    •  
  • Ultimate Ensemble Control: Defines existence itself; reality persists only because his will allows it.
    •  
  • Endless Void: Victims of his Instant Death are erased into a void where even omnipotence cannot function.
    •  
  • Homeostasis: His true form ensures nothing interferes with his human life, deleting threats automatically.
    •  
  • Immortality Cycle: Reborn endlessly after death.
    •  
Other Skills
  • Swordsmanship: Competent with a blade, though vastly overshadowed by his hax abilities.
    •  
  • Disease Immunity: Germs and viruses die near him automatically.
    •  
  • Memory & Concept Killing: Can erase memories or even the idea of something existing.
      

~Now on to the Verdict~

Although Rimuru stands as one of the most broken beings in his verse with reality erasure, law domination, infinite regeneration, and Raphael’s near-omniscience,
Yogiri is on an entirely different level. His Instant Death ability ignores all defenses, immortality, and even causality itself, automatically activating against any threat.
No matter how many times Rimuru resurrects or adapts, Yogiri simply ends his existence, making him the inevitable victor in this matchup.
