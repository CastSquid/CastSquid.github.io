A quick look at our fighter's Strengths!

      
    Physical Abilites
       
  • Immense Strength: Can harm god-tier and planet-level opponents.
    •   
  • Extreme Speed & Reflexes: Moves and reacts faster than light; fights on instinct.
    •   
  • Durability & Endurance: Survives devastating attacks, regenerates stamina mid-battle.
    •   
  • Heightened Senses: Can track, dodge, and counter nearly any attack instinctively.
      
    Energy Abilities
       
  • Ki Mastery:Flight, ki blasts, energy waves.
    •   
  • Instant Transmission: Teleports instantly to sensed energy.
    •   
  • Spirit Control: Advanced ki sensing and manipulation.
    •   
  • Energy Nullification: Can cancel harmful energy effects.
      
    Physical & Combat Mastery
       
  • Martial Arts Genius: Adapts quickly, counters techniques after seeing them once..
    •   
  • Mimicry: Instantly learns and reproduces others’ attacks.
    •   
  • Telepathy: Can read and communicate mind-to-mind.
    •   
  • Spirit Bomb: Can gather massive external energy for a devastating attack.
    •   
  • Signature Moves: Kamehameha, Destructo Disc, Explosive Waves, Soaring Fist.
      
    Mental Abilities
       
  • Ultra Instinct: Automatically dodges and strikes at the most efficient angle without conscious thought.
    •   
  • Tactical Intellect: Creates strategies mid-fight, adapts to opponents rapidly.
