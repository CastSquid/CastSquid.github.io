Quick interview with Sung Jinwoo!

       

Sung jinwoo is he strongest hunter in the world and the successor of the Shadow Monarch.
Once known as the weakest E-Rank, he was chosen by the System and granted limitless growth.
Through relentless battles and mastery of his shadow powers, Jinwoo ascended to become humanity’s greatest protector and a force feared even by Monarchs.
